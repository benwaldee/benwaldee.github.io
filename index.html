Intro

         Hi my name is Ben and I'm a rower, songwriter, gamer, and full stack software engineer. I haven't always been in STEM; I actually dropped out of college freshman year to pursue music with my band. I quickly realized, unfortunately after I had moved across the country, that I enjoyed music as more of a hobby than a career and I came back to college to pursue medicine. I chose medicine because I liked solving problems, and I enjoy fields where you can go endlessly deep in any direction. After college I was accepted into medical school. However, after learning more about the student loan system, and talking to some friends who were not enjoying their time in medical school, I decided that I was most likely working toward a future where I would be unhappy.

         A buddy of mine who was a software engineer recommended I learn how to code because he knew me well and thought I would really like it. So I learned some html and css and spent a weekend building this really bad puzzle website. What I realized is that for the 10 hours I was building this site there was not a second where I didnt feel like my brain was fully engaged. I was hooked and wanted to learn a lot more. I committed to this path in the form of an 80 hour a week 6 month bootcamp, where I learned JS, python, and multiple tools and frameworks that allowed me to build three full stack web applications. My favorite was a goodreads clone where I got to collaborate heavily with 3 other engineers and build out a really complex bookshelf feature. I want to take these skills and work at a company that is committed to tackling problems and building smart solutions, and I'm looking forward to challenging myself and learning more at your company.

         I like to emphasize intuitive front-end page design and powerful, readable backend routes. Check out my projects here.

Crunchyscroll

REPO

Crunchyscroll is a full-stack Crunchyroll clone with a focus on stories instead of videos. Crunchyscroll is built with a Flask/Python/Postgres/SQLAlchemy/Alembic backend and a React/Redux/Javascript frontend. Join a community of short story lovers! Read, post, and save stories to your own crunchylists. Drop reviews on your favorite pieces and vote on which reviews are the most helpful. Reviews are sorted by their "helpfulness" so users can gain insight into what the community thinks about a story at a glance.

Badreads

REPO

Badreads is a full-stack goodreads clone with a literary twist. Badreads is built with a Flask/Python/Postgres/SQLAlchemy/Alembic backend and a React/Redux/Javascript frontend. Find and read the top banned books of yesterday and today; keep track of banned books you want to read and be part of the world's largest community of banned book lovers on badreads. A banned book is a book that has been removed from school curriculums and/or public libraries because a person or group has objected to its content.

AirBnBeep

REPO

AirBnBeep is a streamlined, two-feature web application designed to mimic AirBnb. This project is built with JavaScript,PostgreSQL Sequelize, Express, React, and Redux. It is a home for mobile vacation spots, such as RVs, Airstreams, sprinter-van conversions, and more! Users can host their own spots or review other spots.
